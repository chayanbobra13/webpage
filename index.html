ACCELERATION 2017

A National Level Technical Symposium

"ACCELERATION 2017" a national level event organized by Walchand Institute of Technology Solapur, under the I.S.T.E. Student chapter.
Anybody can hold the helm when the sea is calm but at this ACCELERATION, you will witness a sea full of competitions, which polishes and brings out knowledge, skills and determination within you. With the fields for competitions this year ranging from sustainability and design to advanced robotics to events based on creativity and theatre, ACCELERATION has something in store for everyone. Yes, the time has come when you can prove to the world that you have it in you. No legacy is as rich as that of Competitions at ACCELERATION and we invite all of you to be a part of it. Get ready for the ultimate challenges awaiting you!
